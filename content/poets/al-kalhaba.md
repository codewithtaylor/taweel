# al-Kalḥabah

The origin of the world **al-Kalḥabah**:<br>
"The sound of the fire and it's blaze".

This is a nickname given to him. It is stated that al-Kalḥabah was his mother; if that is correct, then he was nicknamed after his - which is a rare form of attritubtion.

His name was Hubayrah ibn 'Abd Manaf ibn 'Arin ibn Tha'labah ibn Yarbu' ibn Hanzalah ibn Malid ibn Zayd Manah ibn Tamim.

The attritubtion to his grandfather is 'Arīni (with the 'ayn vowelled fathah and retaining the ya). Most scholars refer to him as al-Kalḥabah al-Yurbu'ī.
